# Bread Option

| Required | Type | Multiple |
|--|--|--|
| true | radio | false |

## Modifier Tag

```html
<modifier_bread>
```

## Modifier Details

- **Choose Your Bread**
  - Whole Wheat Ciabatta
  - Whole Wheat Wrap
  - Garlic, Spinach & Pesto Wrap
  - Sundries-Tomato & Basil Wrap
  - No Bread (as a salad)
